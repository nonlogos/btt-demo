MORE THAN JUST SELF- DEFENSE

The benefits of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu go well beyond self-defense.

ANTI-BULLYING

Bullying is a serious problem, and BJJ is an ideal way to empower your child to stand up for and protect themselves if and when needed.

Kiddos will learn valuable self-defense techniques that will help them develop a strong, confident mindset along with the tools they need to nip bullying in the bud.

AGE GROUPS

Our Kids Academy classes are divided by age groups 6-10 and 9-12 years old, allowing children to learn and grow with peers of similar age.

Beginner-friendly and supportive, our more advanced students assist and encourage those new to the program.

We promote respect and do not tolerate negative language.

About BTT Lago Vista

Brazilian Top Team (BTT) Lago Vista is a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Academy offering beginner, intermediate, and advanced classes for both adults and kids.

Led by multiple-time IBJJF champion Prof. Israel Davila, BTT welcomes the Lago Vista and surrounding communities to the Team.

Awaken your inner champion at BTT Lago Vista!
