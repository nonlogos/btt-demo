About BTT Lago Vista

Brazilian Top Team (BTT) Lago Vista is a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Academy offering beginner, intermediate, and advanced classes for both adults and kids.

Led by multiple-time IBJJF champion Prof. Israel Davila, BTT welcomes the Lago Vista and surrounding communities to the Team.

Awaken your inner champion at BTT Lago Vista!
